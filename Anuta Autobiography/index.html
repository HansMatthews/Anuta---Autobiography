OVERVIEW


I was born on August 2, 2003, in a small town in the suburbs. From an early age, I showed a natural talent for contemplation and introspection. I would often spend hours staring out into nature, lost in thought, pondering the mysteries of life. But I'm a lazy person, so I never sought out the reason of it, leaving it just a question.

MUSIC


"I have always found solace and comfort in the beauty of music, and it has been an integral part of my life for as long as I can remember. Whether it's the soothing melodies of sad songs or the loudest screams of pop and rock, I find myself lost in the rhythms and lyrics, allowing my mind to wander and my spirit to soar. For me, there is no greater pleasure than putting on a pair of headphones and immersing myself in a world of sound, letting the music wash over me and transport me to a different time and place."
